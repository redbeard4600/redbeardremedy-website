Reality Check: What Actually Happened

After 12+ years of study and real court testing since 2023, I have the transcripts to prove it. Here's what happens when pseudolegal theories meet real courtrooms.

What Doesn't Work

  • "Legal Fiction" arguments - Judge: "I don't know what you refer to when you say legal fiction"
  • "Special Visitation" claims - Judge: "Can you define what a special visitation is?"
  • Article I vs Article III court confusion - Completely ignored
  • "Maritime Law" and "Not boarding the vessel" - No recognition whatsoever

What Actually Works

  • Due process violations - Courts must respond to procedural errors
  • 14th Amendment protections - Real constitutional law
  • State constitutional protections - Often stronger than federal
  • Actual statutory requirements - Knowing the real law
