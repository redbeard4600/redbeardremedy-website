To exercise these rights, please contact us at the information provided below.

Minors and Educational Access

Educational Content Access: Our educational content may be accessed by individuals of any age who are capable of understanding the material. Legal education and constitutional knowledge should be available to anyone seeking to learn.

No Agreements with Minors: However, we do not enter into any agreements, contracts, or legal relationships with individuals under 18 years of age. This includes but not limited to:

  • Newsletter subscriptions requiring parental consent
  • Any form of consultation or advisory relationships
  • Purchase agreements for materials or services
  • Direct communication regarding personal legal matters

Parental Guidance: We recommend parental supervision for minors accessing legal educational content, as the material discusses complex constitutional and legal concepts that may require adult guidance for proper understanding.
